FAQ

Common questions

Which curriculum does LessonLab follow?

Victorian Curriculum 2.0 — the updated version. All learning intentions and success criteria are written to the current achievement standards, Foundation through Year 6.

Can I edit the lessons after generating?

Yes. Every lesson is fully editable. Adjust the learning intention, success criteria, teaching notes, and any other field before saving or exporting.

What formats can I export to?

Word document (DOCX), PDF, PowerPoint slides, and Compass-compatible format. One click, already formatted.

What's the difference between Monthly and Annual?

Annual costs 31% less, gives you unlimited lesson saves (monthly caps at 20/month), and includes Full Units free. It also matches the school year so you're not thinking about billing mid-term.

Do I need to install anything?

Nothing. LessonLab runs entirely in your browser. Open the app, sign in, and start generating — no download required.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, anytime. Cancel from your account settings with one click. You keep access until the end of your billing period.
