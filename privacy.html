Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 May 2026

LessonLab ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Who we are

LessonLab is operated by Luck Dragon Pty Ltd (ABN 64 697 434 898), an Australian company. Contact us at hello@lessonlab.com.au.

2. Information we collect

  • Account information: your name, email address, and school name when you sign up.
  • Usage data: lesson subjects, year levels, and preferences you enter to generate lesson plans.
  • Payment information: billing is handled entirely by Stripe. We never see or store your card details.
  • Technical data: browser type, device type, and anonymised analytics to improve the service.

3. How we use your information

  • To create and manage your account and subscription.
  • To generate lesson plans using AI (your inputs are sent to AI providers — see below).
  • To send transactional emails (account confirmations, receipts). We do not send marketing emails without consent.
  • To improve LessonLab and fix issues.

4. AI providers and third parties

Lesson generation relies on third-party AI providers (including Anthropic and/or OpenAI). Your lesson inputs (subject, year level, focus) are sent to these providers to generate content. We do not send your name, email, or school name to AI providers. Please review their privacy policies for how they handle submitted data.

Other third parties we use: Stripe (payments), Cloudflare (hosting and security), Resend (transactional email).

5. Data storage and security

Your data is stored on secure servers in the United States (Cloudflare and Supabase infrastructure). We use industry-standard encryption in transit (HTTPS) and at rest. We do not sell your personal information to any third party.

6. Data retention

We retain your account data while your account is active. To request account deletion, use the "Request Account Deletion" button in your Account Settings, or email hello@lessonlab.com.au. We will permanently delete your personal information within 30 days of your request, except where we are required to retain it by law (e.g. financial records for 7 years under Australian tax law).

7. Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by emailing hello@lessonlab.com.au. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Cookies

We use essential cookies to keep you logged in and remember your preferences. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

9. Children

LessonLab is intended for use by teachers and school staff (18+). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Students do not need an account to benefit from LessonLab — it is a teacher planning tool only.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify registered users of material changes by email. Continued use of LessonLab after changes constitutes acceptance.

11. Contact & complaints

For privacy queries or complaints, contact us at hello@lessonlab.com.au. If you are not satisfied with our response, you may contact the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC).
